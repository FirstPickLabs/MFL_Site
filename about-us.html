Our Passion

As of February 2024, MyFantasyLeague is now owned and operated by First Pick Labs, Inc. located in Austin, TX.
Originally, MyFantasyLeague was developed by Sideline Software, Inc. a Wisconsin-based company that was formed in the early 1990’s to help commissioners run their fantasy football leagues.
Our Products
Our premier product is an online fantasy football league management system called MyFantasyLeague.com.
We also had a PC based league management system called “Fantasy Football League Manager” that we offered through the 2007 season.
