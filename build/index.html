Can I customize the rules and scoring settings in my league?
Yes, you have full control over league settings, including scoring rules, roster sizes, draft types, and more. Our platform supports extensive customization to suit your league’s preferences.
How can I draft players in my league?
MyFantasyLeague offers live online drafts, auction drafts, and offline draft support. Commissioners can customize draft settings and conduct drafts according to league preferences.

Yes, you can initiate and negotiate trades with other league members. The platform supports trade proposals, trade review periods, and commissioner approval.
