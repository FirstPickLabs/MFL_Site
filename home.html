Frequently Asked Questions

What types of leagues can I create on MyFantasyLeague?
We support a wide range of league formats, most common are standard redraft, keeper, and dynasty leagues. If your league uses a salary cap each week and does not have a draft, you can setup a DFS style league.

Your league can be as simple or as complex as you want. We support leagues with up to 100 teams, 24 divisions, and 6 conferences. You can also create leagues with multiple player pools, such as deluxe leagues.

We support draft only and guillotine leagues, which have both become very popular. Guillotine leagues work where at the end of each week, the team with the lowest scoring team is eliminated and all the players on that team’s roster are released to free agency. Each format allows for different player management and scoring rules.

We don’t require a credit card and we respect your privacy. Leagues are completely free to use from the preseason all the way through Week 4 of the regular season. There is absolutely no obligation to purchase. We are just that confident that you will enjoy your experience, particularly when you discover all that we have to offer.
Do you offer guillotine, draft only, and DFS style leagues?
Yes, yes and yes! If your league uses a salary cap each week and does not have a draft, you can setup a DFS style league. Draft only and guillotine leagues are among our most popular new league styles.

Guillotine leagues work where at the end of each week, the team with the lowest scoring team is eliminated and all the players on that team’s roster are released to free agency. Each format allows for different player management and scoring rules.

Draft only, or best ball leagues, are leagues where you draft your team and then the computer manages your lineup for you each week. The computer will start the players that score the most points at each position each week. This is a great way to play if you are in a lot of leagues and don’t have time to manage them all.
How much customization can I do with the rules and scoring settings?
You have full control over league settings, including scoring rules, roster sizes, draft types, and more. Our platform supports extensive customization to suit your league’s preferences.
What are the options available for drafting players?
MyFantasyLeague offers live online drafts, auction drafts, and offline draft support. Commissioners can customize draft settings and conduct drafts according to league preferences.

We have a league on another site, can we bring over our league history?
If your league has been around for more than one year, you can keep track of your league’s history on MyFantasyLeague.com. Each season that you host your league with us, the league data is automatically kept on-line and accessible for you to refer back to indefinitely. You can even transfer your history from another site or from your paper records to keep track of it on MyFantasyLeague.com. And there are several historical reports that you can use to view Franchise and League Records, such as the highest score of the year for all past seasons, the widest margin of victory, the highest scoring players at each position, and even the cumulative series record for each franchise in the league.
Do you support very large leagues with multiple conferences and player pools?
Yes, definitely! We call leagues with multiple player pools "deluxe" leagues. See this page for a detailed description of deluxe leagues, but first, determine if you need a deluxe league. If you truly need multiple player pools AND these player pools are separated by conference or division, then the answer is yes. Otherwise, you do not need a deluxe league.
